Got questions?

How do I create a playlist?

To create a playlist, go to Your Library and tap Create Playlist.

How do I activate Data Saver mode?

You can turn on Data Saver from Settings to save mobile data while streaming.

Is it only possible to shuffle play music?

No, premium users can play any song on demand without shuffle mode.

Where can I find Podcasts?

You can find podcasts in the Search tab or in Your Library.
